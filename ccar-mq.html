Charlie's Chance Animal Rescue was my final assignment for my first college course at DCCC. The content, design, and subject were all my own choices. With only a few specific requirements, this first project was a challenge. We did not use frameworks, simply media queries and a little bit of Flexbox. It taught me to prefer doing work the hard way. My coding experience prior to college was a Codeacademy Intensive Freelance Developer program, in addition to other free Codeacademy courses and working on Wordpress for local organizations. Creating Charlie's Chance Animal Rescue proved to be a challenge and an inpiration, a conformation of the wisdom of my new choice to become a developer.
